## Lunch with Kela card

TAI meal services make use of the Kela card. Students must read their card to verify their right to a free meal at their school building’s cafeteria. To avoid queues, please follow the meal times indicated by your teacher in charge. 

## Special diets

The guidelines and notification practices of the City of Turku are used for special diets. You can check the suitability of an individual dish in the product information provided by the school restaurant.
