How to start using the Slice student card:

1) Authorisation: Authorise Slice to deliver your digital student card on Wilma. Permission to transfer data can be found under Forms > Personal data form > My data may be transferred to the Slice student card service. Your permission will be transferred to Slice every day at noon, so please wait until that has been done before you go to step 2.

2) Upload a photo: Next day, go to slice.fi/upload/tai to upload your photo for the student card. If necessary, contact info@slice.fi.

3) Card activation: After uploading the photo, you will receive the activation codes for your student card in your email shortly. Download the Slice.fi application from the app store and log in to the email with your IDs.

The card is now ready – take advantage of your student benefits and enjoy your studies at TAI!

To change your student card to a new phone:

If you change phones, you can reset your user ID at slice.fi/resetoi. In the form, enter the e-mail address you entered for Slice. You will receive a link to your email shortly which you can click to receive the new app activation code in your email.

More information: slice.fi

In case of problems, primarily contact Slice’s customer service: info@slice.fi.

If you are unable to resolve your problem through Slice’s customer service, contact your institute’s student affairs office.
