## Description of the Training

Study restaurant and catering in a practical way. The employment situation in the industry is good. A cook who has completed the competence area of food services works at restaurants or in the catering sector, staff canteens, cafés, catering companies, freight and passenger ships as well as institutional kitchens.

Qualification holders are able to
• plan and carry out food preparation or customer service tasks and work in sales in the catering sector
• work in a sales-minded, customer service oriented and quality conscious manner and profitably, productively, and responsibly as well as operate in compliance with hygiene requirements, and sustainable operating practices.

A cook who has completed the qualification knows how to
• work in food preparation tasks at restaurants with different business ideas or missions or in public sector units
• prepare tasty, nutritious, and wholesome food, taking special diets into account
• set out food as individual plated dishes or dishes for a large group of customers.

The training lasts approximately 1,5-2 years or proceeds according to a personal plan. The aim of the training is to complete the Vocational Qualification in Restaurant and Catering Service in its entirety. The training is full-time.

The training includes contact teaching, distance learning and on-the-job learning. Contact teaching is 5 days per week between 8:00 and 16:00. At other times, you carry out distance learning as well as on-the-job learning in accordance with regular working hours.

Holidays:

The scope of the Vocational Qualification in Restaurant and Catering Services is 180 competence points.

The training itself is free of charge. Acquisition of working clothes and working footwear incur expenses.

## Applying

Application for the training opens on April 28th 2025.

Apply for the training program by filling in the online application form carefully and by attending the mandatory admissions info and interview.

Only eligible applicants will be invited to the mandatory admissions info. Invitations are sent by email. In addition to your email inbox, frequently check your spam folder.

Mandatory admissions info will be held on Tuesday, September 4th, 2025 at the school. The interview day begins with a general information session about the studies, followed by a written task and an individual interview.

Students are selected from applicants who meet the admission criteria. All applicants will be notified of admissions.
