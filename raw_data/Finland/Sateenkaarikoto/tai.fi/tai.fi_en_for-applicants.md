## Would you like to learn a profession? Or do you want to increase your skills?

Below, you can find more information about studying and applying for a place at TAI.

### Joint application

The joint application process for upper secondary education will be available between 18 February and 18 March 2025. See what TAI has to offer and learn your first profession here with us!

### Incomplete degree

Do you want to complete an incomplete degree or would you like to increase your competence? Do you feel that your competence is sufficient for a direct demonstration? Or do you want to transfer from another educational institution?

Please fill in the enquiry form and its appendices, and our guidance counsellor will contact you as soon as possible. You can also contact a field-specific guidance counsellor or our Guidance and Application Service directly.
