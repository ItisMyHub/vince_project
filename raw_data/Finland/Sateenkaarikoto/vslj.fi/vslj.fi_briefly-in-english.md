# Child Welfare Organisations of Southwest Finland

Child Welfare Organisations of Southwest Finland is an advocate and promoter of child protection and children's rights. We strive to improve the prerequisites for operation of child welfare organisations. We work with an aim for having societal impact and our work is based on knowledge and research. In addition, we develop, implement and pilot service concepts. Child Welfare Organisations of Southwest Finland brings together the child welfare organisations in the area as well as their expertise.
