### Further studies

Do you want to pursue further studies? Here are some options:

- Further and specialist vocational qualifications strengthen your vocational competence and offer you an opportunity to specialise in your field of study. See the training programmes open for application at TAI.
- Changing the field of study? You can apply to study for a vocational upper secondary qualification through continuous admission. See training programmes.
- Universities of applied sciences and universities offer extensive study opportunities. Also remember Open University of Applied Sciences and Open University. The joint application process is arranged in spring and autumn. The application can be submitted via the Opintopolku.fi website.
### Employment

If you need help in finding employment, the employment services of your municipality of residence will offer support and advice.

- Employment services of the City of Turku: Information for jobseekers and employees
